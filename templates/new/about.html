38 Year Authority in Real Estate Development & Investing

Ignite your investing career now!

With 38 years of residential and commercial Real Estate development experience, 22 years as an Investor and Coach, negotiating over 300 short sales, Bill has mastered every tactic to making money in Real Estate. Bill, Founder and CEO of B.H. Coaching, is now living the American Dream. He is a self-made millionaire, but the journey from the streets of Jersey City to becoming a recognized authority on Real Estate Investing has not been easy. He has attended and graduated from the “School of Hard Knocks”. He gained his experience through perseverance, working hard, and by remaining focused and determined. Bill has a tremendous passion for what he does, which he believes is most important, since without passion you have no energy, and without energy you will not be successful. Bill’s success can be directly attributed to his passion for his work, his energy to pursue opportunities, his never ending quest to discover new and creative solutions for his clients, and his willingness to help people achieve their dreams. Bill is a family man and a man of faith. He possesses a sincere desire to share his experiences, strategies, and secrets, so that others can achieve the same success!
